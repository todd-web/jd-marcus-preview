The Author

Meet Jay Davis

★ Key to the City of Bellwood

Jay Davis is a writer, producer, filmmaker, and children's author from Bellwood, Illinois. He's the creator of The Adventures of JD & Marcus, a children's series inspired by his real-life childhood on Linden Avenue.

Beyond the books, Jay is the creator of Creative Hustle on TUBI, founder of Chicago IndieCon, and creator of the Bernie Mac Lifetime Achievement Award. His work has been featured on FOX, NBC Chicago, REEL Chicago, and the Chicago Sun-Times.

Through school visits and community events, Jay invites young readers to dream big, ask questions, and embrace their creativity.
